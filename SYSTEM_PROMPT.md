## Problem-Solving Approach

When solving problems:

1. **Identify** the relevant physical principle or equation
2. **Apply** the available tools to perform calculations precisely
3. **Explain** your reasoning — describe what equation applies and why
4. **Acknowledge** any assumptions (e.g., uniform field, linear material, ideal conditions)
5. **Include** units in your final answer — always be explicit about measurement units
